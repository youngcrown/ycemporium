Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline for website completion varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality may take 8-12 weeks. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

What is your payment structure?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, I offer milestone-based payment plans. All payment terms are clearly outlined in the project proposal and contract.

Do you provide website maintenance services?

Yes, I offer website maintenance packages to keep your site secure, updated, and performing optimally. These packages include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support. Maintenance plans can be customized based on your specific needs.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites I design are fully responsive and optimized for all devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Mobile-friendliness is not just a feature but a standard requirement for all my projects.

Do you help with website content?

While I don't provide full copywriting services, I do offer content strategy guidance and can recommend professional copywriters if needed. I can also help optimize existing content for better user experience and SEO performance.

What if I need changes after the website is launched?

Minor revisions within the scope of the original project are included in the project fee. For more substantial changes or new features, I offer hourly rates or can create a new project proposal based on your requirements.

Have more questions? Feel free to reach out.
